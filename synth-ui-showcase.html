synth-ui — component showcase

Every tactile control at min / mid / max and on / off. Drag knobs vertically, hold faders, flip switches, sweep the pad. Double-tap a knob or fader to reset.

Knob

Rotary look, indicator + tick marks, depth. Vertical drag = value. The Filter knob glows open as it rises.

Fader

Grabbable cap you hold and drag; fill tracks the value.

Switch

Rocker with a flip/clunk on toggle.

XY pad

Visible puck with a fading trail — for the keyboard's X (pitch) / Y (timbre) expression. Sweep it.
